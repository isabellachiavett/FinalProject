About Me

I wrote this in January. I believe it still holds strong in representing who I am.

who am i? well my friends call me “Isabella”; my family only refer to me as “Nena” my boyfriend has the privilege of “amor” and while that is “who I am” it only captivates my flesh and blood; not the soul behind it all

isabella chiavettone: a hardworking; college graduate at only twenty years old!!! i can hear my moms voice as i write this knowing that is how she sees me i am grateful that those who love me see me that way knowing there was a point where i held resentment towards those who placed expectations on me and while i still hold myself to certain expectations i know that i; and everyone else around me, is doing the best with what we have got

that’s what life is, isn’t it? a contradiction often comprised of the battle between “who i am” and “what i want to be” college right! a time where i am supposed to figure out my future career i still don’t know what i want to do and i’m still working at the same coffee shop since i was 16

that is all who i could be, what i could believe but i wake up every day knowing that i am apart of a reality much bigger my actions affect not only me, but my community; my planet in my personal pursuit of pleasure i found serving others truly fulfills me

if 16 year old isabella were to read this she would be underwhelmed with who she became thank goodness for maturing! (okay this was pretty extreme 16 y.o. me wasn’t that blind with privilege)

so isabella chiavettone; who i really am: i am a spiritual; dependable; supportive; loving; authentic individual i am working towards feeding my soul and most importantly; i am doing my best with what i got
